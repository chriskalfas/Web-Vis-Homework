    • Latitude Exploration: A Precipitation Study

    This project was designed to analyze and visualize the data based on the amount of precipitation as one gets further from the equator.

    This was achieved by pulling data from the OpenWeatherMapAPI and assembling a dataset on over 500 cities.

    Once the dataset was created, Matplotlib was used to plot various aspects of interest. Factors we looked at included: Temperature, Cloudiness, Wind Speed, and Humidity. This site provides the source data and visualizations created

    This site provides the different visualizations created and access to the data used.
